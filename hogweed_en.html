Hogweed Productions was founded by students at the Norwegian university NTNU in the fall of 2021, as part of a school project to make a web based game.

We chose Heracleum mantegazzianum as our emblem. The giant hogweed is a beautiful flower native to the Caucasian hills: It can grow to about five meters, is highly invasive, and has a poison that leaves severe chemical burns.

Hogweed is a two person project consisting of:


Petter "so I've tinkered around a bit" Jørgensen

Petter was born in 1999 and grew up in a small town outside Bodø. He has been interested in computers all of his life, and have spent the last few years as a student of technology at NTNU. He is currently living and studying in Gjøvik.


Simen "or some little bastard on a pogo stick" Hagerup

Simen was born in 1980 and works with literature. He has previously studied in Bø, Copenhagen, Paris and Stockholm; this time, he is dead set on completing a Bachelor's degree.
