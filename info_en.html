Daxtrot

by Hogweed Productions

Daxtrot is a game made by students at NTNU as a group project in the autumn of 2021. Daxtrot is played by two players on the same computer or mobile phone. The goal of the game is to help two frogs riding on a dachshund, through a perilous landscape.
